About Us :

The core objective of an academic library is to fulfil the information needs of its stakeholders by making available predominant sources of information and provision of information services. As it has been presumed as the heart of every institution, the overall health of the parent institution depends on it.
DYPIU’s “Learning Resource Plaza” (Central Library) holds the rich collection of text as well as reference books in the domain of Computer Science, Biotechnology, Biomedical Engineering, Design, Management, Pharmacy, Engineering and Technology and allied domains of knowledge.
It’s collection comprise of printed documents such as Books, Research Journals, Technical Magazines, Project Reports etc. ; whereas E- Books, E-Journals, E-Thesis, E-proceedings and other E-Databases, CDs/DVDs etc. are hosting the e-resources collection counterpart.
